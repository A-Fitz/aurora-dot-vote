Aurora.vote is

a central hub of election information for residents of Aurora, Illinois.

the only source of interactive maps that track all units of local government which serve Aurorans.

a central directory of candidates that Aurorans can vote on in the upcoming election.

Get registered to vote on your county website or at elections.il.gov.

Vision for Aurora.vote:

  • A database of information about elected officials that is not often cataloged for many units of local government
  • A place for candidates to provide information about themselves, centralizing information for voters and allowing candidates to avoid costly website development
  • A non-partisan non-profit that increases voter participation through grassroots efforts by identifying key areas of voter nonparticipation and incentivizing neighborhood remediative actions

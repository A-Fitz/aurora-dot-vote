Aurora.vote is a central hub of election information for residents of Aurora, Illinois.

Aurora.vote is the only source of interactive maps that track all units of local government which serve Aurorans.

Aurora.vote has the only directory of all candidates that Aurorans can vote on in the upcoming election.

My vision for Aurora.vote:

  • A database of information about elected officials that is not often cataloged for many units of local government
  • A place for candidates to provide information about themselves, centralizing information for voters and allowing candidates to avoid costly website development
  • A non-partisan non-profit that increases voter participation through grassroot efforts by identifying key areas of voter nonparticipation and incentivizing neighboorhood remediative actions

Please note that all information on this website is unofficial. For official information please visit your respective county elections website:

2025 Election Dates

February 25, 2025 - Consolidated Primary

Early voting is open until February 24th! Learn more on your respective county elections website.

February 25th is a Tuesday. During the Consolidated Primary Election, you vote to narrow the field of candidates that will be presented on the April 1, 2025 Consolidated Election ballot. The following races may be on your Consolidated Primary ballot if you live in Aurora.

  • City of Aurora
    • Mayor (narrow to 2 candidates)
  • Aurora Township - Democratic Party
    • Clerk (narrow to 1 candidate)
    • Highway Commissioner (narrow to 1 candidate)
    • Trustee (narrow to 4 candidates)

April 1, 2025 - Consolidated Election

April 1st is a Tuesday. Learn how to vote early, by mail, or in-person on your respective county elections website. See the maps below to learn which candidates may be on your ballot.

Important Dates

  • March 7, 2025 (Friday): First Day of Early Voting and Voting by Mail
  • March 27, 2025 (Thursday): Last Day to Mail Vote by Mail Ballots for the Consolidated Election
  • March 31, 2025 (Monday): Last Day for Early Voting for the Consolidated Election

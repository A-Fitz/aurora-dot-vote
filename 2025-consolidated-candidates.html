Your Civic Involvement

I have volunteered with various organizations throughout my life. I was an AmeriCorp member, stayed on with the organization as a freelancer after my corps year to continue serving communities throughout the US. My family always valued civic engagement, we volunteered with organizations that ranged from delivering meals to elderly to renovating open spaces for community use. My husband and I believe in service. He is a combat veteran and graduate of West Point who now serves as an Assist. State's Attorney in Kane County. We are members of Holy Cross Catholic Church. We moved here for family, believing a strong family unit is the best gift - to each other and the community. Living in Batavia for a year, I know this is one of the best places in the United States to raise a family. I really want everyone here to feel that.

Why Are You Running?

My neighbors and friends asked me to. My background is in business. I've worked as a filmmaker for the last 15 years. I started 3 successful companies, and have consulted for many others. I know how to allocate funds, understand the needs of departments, and work with a diverse team to support the "whole operation". My goal is not as a disruptor, but to work with the current board members to best serve the day to day operations and most importantly the taxpayers. I am focused on the safety of our next generation of citizens, providing transparent communication between citizens and the board. The citizens in Batavia have expressed their concerns on various topics, and I will make sure those are heard and all issues - especially those directly affecting children - are addressed.
